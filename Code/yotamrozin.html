I’ve built motion identities, campaigns, and award-winning digital and print products for 100+ brands—from Microsoft to the EU—trusted by global studios, industry giants, and start ups alike.

Notable Clients

As Peanuts' Creative Lead, I built Payoneer's complete motion system—from micro-interactions to form submissions—keeping millions of users engaged across every touchpoint.

As Peanuts' Creative Lead, I directed Melio's explainer—a bill pay platform that streamlined SMB payments before Xero acquired them. Nice to contribute to a billion-dollar exit story.

Created visual identity and website for body-based psychotherapist Matilda Tonkin Wells—emphasizing the spaciousness and care of her practice. Her handmade illustrations became the heart of the visual world to bring in the playfulness, intimacy, and creativity of the work.

As Head of Creative at PlayGen, I pioneered AI integration in game art production, securing partnerships with Playtika and Unity's Supersonic. The example shows our streamlined meta-layer asset pipeline for casual games—turning algorithm chaos into production gold

Created an explainer for Sweeps (Y Combinator → Cloudflare acquisition) that makes data validation sexy. Transformed 'AI-powered analytics cleanup' into something founders (and investors) actually wanted to watch

Designed and built this portfolio for double AOP Award winner Rocio Chacon—a photographer who finds beauty in overlooked moments. The site's breathing rhythm seeks to reflect her dance-informed approach to capturing life.

Led R&D into AI pipeline development, solving consistency challenges across styling, posing, rigging, and more.

Led the creative development of an end-to-end AI asset pipeline for Supersonic's 'Summer Camp Merge.' —automation never looked so wholesome. We turned nostalgia into scalable game art for gameworld assets and merge chain items.

Home makeover meets machine learning. Led asset creation for 'Match it All!'—our AI pipeline turned basic match-3 room decoration into algorithmically perfected interior design.

Created complete visual identity and developed web presence for thisPlace, a trauma-informed dance and theatre company based in Wales, UK. Designed clarity and accessibility into every element while emphasizing a creative, safe, inclusive, and nurturing environment.

Beyond client work, I’ve donated design, animation, and post-production to movements I care about—from Peace Now and the Geneva Initiative, to refugee support at the Tibet Hope Center, to animal rights advocacy with Let the Animals Live.

Led global team of creatives to create an animated short for activist duo The Yes Men—a subversive call-to-action that earned official selections at four international animation festivals. Activism meets animation direction

Created campaign materials across industries—tech startups, creative studios, artists, cultural festivals and more. Featured: Payoneer's Social Media post for International Pride Day—financial services meet rainbow solidarity.

Brought The5ers website to life with Peanuts Studio—interactive hero animations, loaders, and icon packs for a forex fund that actually knows what they're doing. Because these guys deserve better design than most crypto bros get.

Developed an explainer video for the Munters Rotem Trio, a climate control system that makes poultry and swine farms run smoother than most startups. Precision agriculture, visualized
